Bachelor of Software Engineering


Programme code: N/481/6/0837 (11/26) MQA/PA 15007
Mode of study: Full Time
Duration: 3 years
Graduating credit: 120
Intakes: January ,March, and August

Introduction

Software engineering is a discipline that involves the application of scientific and engineering principles, problem-solving and analytical skills by writing code to develop software for multiple platforms. Our programme structure encourages creativity, uses current industry software tools and teaches the latest techniques in software development, testing, deployment and project management as well as quality management.

Objective

Students will learn the essential elements of software engineering and gain invaluable experience in teamwork, project management, the utilisation of industry best practice techniques, industry internship, and be involved in a major software-related project.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the Software Engineering Programme would be able to have careers both in the government and private sector including as: . Software Programmer
. IT Assistant Executive
. Software Technical Support Officer
. Software Sales Representative
. Software Tester
. Software Architect
. Software Development Consultant
. Software Engineer
. Software Developers
. Software Project Manager

Requirements

A pass in Matriculation or Foundation studies or equivalent with minimum CGPA of 2.00 or a pass in STPM or equivalent with a minimum Grade C (GP 2.00) in any 2 subjects and credits in the following subjects at SPM level or its equivalent:
. Additional Mathematics; or
. Mathematics and one of the Science, Technology or Engineering related subject.
Note: Candidates for category from the last criteria as initially mentioned must take mathematics strengthening subject with topics that are appropriate to the field of Computer Science and Software Engineering at the beginning of their study.
Students are required to pass the Mathematics strengthening subject as a prerequisite before being allowed to take the relevant core courses. However, this does not prevent students from pursuing other core or non-core courses which do not set the mathematics strengthening subject as a prerequisite. The requirement for Mathematics strengthening subject can be exempted if the Matriculation programme, Foundation studies or equivalent has offered mathematics subject and their achievements are equivalent or more than the credit requirements of the Additional Mathematics at SPM level; OR
. A pass in STPM Science stream or equivalent with a minimum Grade C (GP 2.00) in one Mathematics subject and one Science/ICT subject; OR
. A Diploma in Computer Science / Software Engineering / Information Technology / Information Systems or equivalent with a minimum CGPA of 2.50; OR
. Any other Diploma in science and technology with a minimum CGPA of 2.50.
Note: Candidates for categories iii and iv with a CGPA between 2.00 and 2.50 may be admitted subject to a rigorous internal assessment process of the HEP.
